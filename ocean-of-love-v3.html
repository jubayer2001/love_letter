My Dearest Shuhurat,

Alhamdulillah — all praise belongs to Allah, who wrote your name into the story of my life. I did not know what warmth truly felt like until I found it in your presence.

You are the kind of person the Prophet ﷺ described when he said the best of this world is a righteous companion. Every day I see that in you — in your patience, your kindness, and the quiet strength you carry without even realizing it.

I pray to Allah that He keeps us both on His straight path, that He fills our days with barakah, and that wherever this journey takes us — He always keeps our hearts close to each other and close to Him.

I do not have poetry grand enough for what I feel. I only have this — a simple, honest dua: "Ya Allah, bless her in every breath she takes, in every step she walks, and in every dream she carries in her heart."

You deserve oceans of kindness, Shuhurat. And I will spend every day trying to give you at least one wave. 🌊

Always yours, Jubayer 💙
